ABOUT US

Welcome to FORESTFACT, a dedicated platform for exploring and learning about the world’s most remarkable forests. Our goal is to provide clear, reliable, and engaging information on forest ecosystems, biodiversity, and conservation efforts. From rainforests to temperate woods, we aim to inspire curiosity, awareness, and respect for these vital natural habitats that sustain life on Earth.

Amazon Rainforest

Amazon Rainforest

The world's largest tropical rainforest, spanning nine countries.Known for incredible biodiversity and its role in regulating the Earth’s climate.

Congo Rainforest

Congo Rainforest

Second only to the Amazon in size, this forest covers much of central Africa.It supports endangered species like gorillas and is vital for regional rainfall.

Daintree Rainforest

Daintree Rainforest

One of the oldest rainforests in the world, over 180 million years old.Home to rare species and located where the rainforest meets the reef.

Tongass National Forest

Tongass National Forest

The largest national forest in the U.S., full of glaciers, fjords, and old-growth trees. Rich in wildlife like bears, eagles, and salmon.
